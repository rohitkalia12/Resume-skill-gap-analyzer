Skill Preparation Schedule

Resume Skill Gap Analyzer - Gen AI Squad

Week 1: Foundation Building

Duration: 10 hours

  • Review resume analysis results and identify key skill gaps
  • Set up learning environment (install necessary tools and software)
  • Study fundamental concepts related to your target job role
  • Complete beginner-level tutorials and exercises

Week 2: Skill Development

Duration: 12 hours

  • Dive deeper into missing skills from resume analysis
  • Practice coding problems or relevant exercises
  • Build small projects to apply new knowledge
  • Participate in online coding challenges or quizzes

Week 3: Project Building

Duration: 15 hours

  • Start working on a portfolio project related to your job role
  • Implement features using newly learned skills
  • Debug and optimize your code
  • Document your project and learning process

Week 4: Advanced Practice

Duration: 12 hours

  • Solve real-world problems and case studies
  • Contribute to open-source projects or collaborate with peers
  • Review and refactor your previous projects
  • Explore advanced topics in your field

Week 5: Interview Preparation

Duration: 10 hours

  • Practice technical interview questions
  • Prepare answers for behavioral interview questions
  • Mock interviews with friends or mentors
  • Update resume and portfolio with new projects

Week 6: Final Review & Application

Duration: 8 hours

  • Review all learned skills and concepts
  • Apply for jobs that match your improved skill set
  • Network with professionals in your target industry
  • Continue learning and stay updated with industry trends

Total Preparation Time: 67 hours
